Building a Smarter Hiring Process with Recruit Sence (ATS)

Published on October 6, 2025

One of my most rewarding projects was developing Recruit Sence, an Applicant Tracking System (ATS). I was driven to create a more efficient hiring process, and my goal was to significantly reduce the time recruiters spend on initial resume screening.

The core of this project was a Machine Learning model that I deployed. Using Natural Language Processing (NLP), the model could analyze resumes and job descriptions to find the best candidate matches. This wasn't just about matching keywords; the model understood the context of skills and experience.

The results were impressive: The model achieved an accuracy of over 85%, which directly led to a 60% reduction in manual screening time for recruiters. This not only made their job easier but also improved the hiring success rate for over 500 successful matches.

This project taught me the immense power of combining Python and Machine Learning to solve a clear business problem. It solidified my belief in the potential of AI to automate and optimize everyday tasks, leading to more efficient and successful outcomes.
